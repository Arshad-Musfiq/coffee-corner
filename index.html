Espresso

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuchino

A coffee drink that combines equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a creamy and frothy texture.

Latte

A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, often topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Mocha

A chocolate-flavored variant of a latte, typically made with espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, and sometimes topped with whipped cream. Macchiato

Macchiato

An espresso-based drink with a small amount of milk or milk foam added, creating a layered appearance.

Americano

A coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water, resulting in a similar strength to traditional brewed coffee.

Flat White

A coffee drink originating from Australia and New Zealand, consisting of espresso and steamed milk with a thin layer of microfoam on top.

Irish Coffee

A cocktail consisting of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and topped with cream, often served in a glass mug.

Affogato

A dessert or coffee drink made by pouring a shot of hot espresso over a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato.

Turkish Coffee

A traditional method of brewing coffee by boiling finely-ground coffee beans with water and sometimes sugar, served unfiltered and often with grounds settled at the bottom of the cup.
